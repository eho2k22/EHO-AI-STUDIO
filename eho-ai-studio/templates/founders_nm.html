Ernie Ho - Founder

*Ernie Ho received his Bachelors in Computer Science from UC Berkeley, and Masters of Engineering in Computer Science from Cornell.
*Ernie had held a variety of application software developer positions from EFI, Siebel, Oracle before moving on to
a variety of technical consulting roles at Oracle, Greensoft prior to transitioning to taking on the VP of Marketing role at CybEye, an app platform company building custom white-labeled web2/web3 solutions for SMB across industries.
*For the past year, Ernie has been researching, testing AI development tools and APIs
to build unique AI generative content applications.
*In December 2022 Ernie Ho founded EHO AI STUDIO No 23 with a mission to build a versatile and engaging AI Bot that
produces qualified and reliable and educational responses to queries rangning from life hacks, to complex requirements that
require technical and abstract understanding, exceptional problem solving and comunication skills,
all the while staying objective, respectful and enjoyable.
*Ernie firmly believes that with powerful AI tools now already doing the heavy lifting,
the ball is in our (humans) court to inject ingenuity, bestow meaning, and create value through innovative applications at scale.
