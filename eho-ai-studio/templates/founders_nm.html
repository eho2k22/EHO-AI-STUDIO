Promptlys.ORG FOUNDER

Ernie Ho - Founder

Ernie Ho earned his Bachelors in Computer Science from UC Berkeley, and Masters of Engineering in Computer Science from Cornell.

Ernie has had 10+ years software development experience, and held multiple application software developer positions from EFI, Siebel, to Oracle, before transitioning to
technical consulting roles at Oracle, Greensoft, then later taking on the VP of Product Strategy role at CybEye, an app platform solution provider building custom white-labeled social app solutions for SMB across industries.

For the past 12 months Ernie Ho discovered the integrability of AIGC tools and APIs and developed a strong interest in
leveraging AIGC content to scale social platforms.

In December 2022 Ernie Ho founded EHO AI STUDIO No 23 with a mission to build a versatile and engaging AI Bot that
produces qualified, reliable and educational responses to queries rangning from simple life hacks, to complex business requirements
all the while staying objective, respectful and enjoyable.

In January 2023 Promptlys.ORG was launched to allow users to share their prompts with others as a new way to interact, socialize, build meaningful connections.

Ernie firmly believes with powerful AI tools now already doing the heavy lifting,
the ball is in our (humans) court to inject ingenuity, bestow meaning, and create value through innovative applications at scale.
