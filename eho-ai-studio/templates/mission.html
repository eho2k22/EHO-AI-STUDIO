Promptlys MISSION STATEMENT


Promptlys - Prompt Sharing Social Platform

Promptlys is a Prompt Social platform that enables Prompt Masters to create original prompts, share or sell their premium prompts, and allow other members to benefit from their prompts. By sharing and mutually benefitting from effective prompts, users can improve own prompt engineering efficiency as well as participating in a collaborative prompt sharing community.

Quality prompts are essential for producing quality AIGC content, and Promptlys provides Prompt Masters with the tools they need to create high-quality prompts that will produce accurate and reliable content.

Promptlys platform provides a forum for Prompt Masters to share their expertise and collaborate on projects. This allows Prompt Masters to socialize and learn from one another to hone their prompt skills.

The market for quality prompts is growing rapidly. The global AI market is expected to reach $702.12 billion by 2028, and the demand for quality prompts is only going to increase. Our platform is well-positioned to capitalize on this growth.

We plan to generate revenue through a subscription model. Prompt Masters can subscribe to our platform to access our tools and resources. We also offer a premium subscription that includes additional features and benefits.

We have already signed up over 100 Prompt Masters to our platform and currently in the beta testing phase and we are expecting to launch our platform to the public in the next few months.

Our team is composed of experienced professionals with a passion for AI. We have a strong track record of success in the technology industry.

We are seeking investment to help us launch our platform to the public. We believe that our platform has the potential to be a major success and we are excited to partner with investors who share our vision.
